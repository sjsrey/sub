PySAL 1.8 Released

Date Fri 25 July 2014 Tags pysal

We normally ship PySAL on a six-month release cycle, once at the end of January and again the end of July. This year due to travel schedules (it is summer after all) we shipped 1.8 one week early.

A lot of work on the testing infrastructure, bug fixes and optimizations. One new contrib module that provides for interaction between PySAL and clusterpy.
